Chinese cuisine

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture, which includes cuisine originating from the divers regions of China, as well as from Chinese people in oter parts of the world. Because of the Chinese diaspora and historical powe of the country, Chinese cuisine has ifulenced many other cuisines in Asia, with modifications made to cater to local palates. Chines food staples such as rice,soy sauce, noodles, tea, and tofu, and utensils such as chopsticks and wok, can be found worldwide.
